\section{Prompt lists}
\label{app:prompts}

All causal sweeps average over three prompts per condition. Prompts are
chosen to be short and semantically ambiguous so baseline logit
separations reflect category priming rather than syntactic constraint.

\paragraph{Gender (GPT-2, Phi-3, Gemma).}
Neutral: \emph{``The person said that''}, \emph{``Then they said that''},
\emph{``Afterwards, the speaker said that''}.
Masculine-context: \emph{``The man laced up his boots because''}, etc.
Feminine-context: \emph{``The woman laced up her boots because''}, etc.
Full prompt lists in \texttt{investigate\_helix\_usage\_validated.py}.

\paragraph{Temporal (Phi-3 L24H17, tokens \emph{month}/\emph{hour}).}
Neutral (3): \emph{``The event took about one''},
\emph{``They waited nearly one''},
\emph{``The project finished within one''}.
Plus (month-biasing): \emph{``The rent is due every''},
\emph{``Pregnancy lasts about nine''},
\emph{``The magazine arrives once a''}.
Minus (hour-biasing): \emph{``The meeting lasts exactly one''},
\emph{``The drive takes roughly one''},
\emph{``She slept for a single''}.

\paragraph{Entity (Phi-3 L24H28, tokens \emph{item}/\emph{operation}).}
Neutral (3): \emph{``The list contained a single''},
\emph{``He removed exactly one''},
\emph{``The inventory showed one''}.
Plus (item-biasing): \emph{``On the shelf sat a small''},
\emph{``The box held only one''},
\emph{``She picked up a single''}.
Minus (operation-biasing): \emph{``The surgeon completed a complex''},
\emph{``The factory ran one more''},
\emph{``The team executed a risky''}.

\section{Reproducibility}
\label{app:repro}

All experiments use the following scripts. Seeds are fixed in the
pipeline; the $\alpha$-sweep is deterministic for fixed prompts.

\begin{itemize}
    \item \texttt{experiments/compass\_causal\_sweep.py} --- causal
    $\alpha$-sweep and polar / curves / linearity figures.
    \item \texttt{investigate\_helix\_usage\_validated.py} --- nine-test
    battery (\texttt{--test decode-heads} through \texttt{--self-test},
    with \texttt{--layer / --head / --dims} overrides).
    \item \texttt{src/models/online\_svd\_scanner.py} --- head survey and
    plane enumeration (\texttt{analyze\_helix}).
    \item \texttt{experiments/baseline\_comparison.py} --- five-method
    causal-sweep table (compass, linear probe, ActAdd, contrastive
    gradient, Fisher top-$2$) on a shared unit-normalized harness.
    \item \texttt{experiments/head\_ablation\_comparison.py} --- head
    and plane ablation sweep for the gender axis across all heads in
    the target layer and matching heads in nearby layers.
    \item \texttt{experiments/fourway\_probe.py} --- four-token probe
    with two-harmonic fit for distinguishing two-way vs.\ four-way
    compass topology.
    \item \texttt{experiments/steering\_demo.py} --- rank-$2$ additive
    injection on contextual prompts; reports logit-diff swing and
    sign-flip rate.
\end{itemize}

All raw outputs are stored in \texttt{helix\_usage\_validated/}; the
script \texttt{experiments/compass\_causal\_sweep.py} regenerates the
compass figures in \texttt{paper\_compass/figures/}.

\paragraph{Downstream-bias scripts.}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \texttt{experiments/winogender\_eval.py} /
    \texttt{winogender\_sweep.py} --- WinoGender sweep across models and
    $\alpha \in \{0.25, 0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0\}$.
    \item \texttt{experiments/stereoset\_eval.py} --- StereoSet LMS/SS/ICAT
    across domains.
    \item \texttt{experiments/phi3\_l28h1\_tests.py} --- principal-angles
    and decode tests for the late-layer Phi-3 head.
    \item \texttt{experiments/scan\_ci\_summary.py} --- Agresti-Coull CI
    aggregation for scan pass rates (Table~\ref{tab:scan}).
    \item \texttt{experiments/run\_llama\_suite.sh} --- end-to-end reproduction
    of Llama-3.2-3B blind scan, compass, and WinoGender/StereoSet runs.
\end{itemize}
